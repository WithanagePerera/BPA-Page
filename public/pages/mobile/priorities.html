Priorities



Finances

Praveen believes that costs are the biggest issue impacting chapters and the opportunities of BPA delegates. That is why it is the foremost issue on his agenda and something he hopes to alleviate as a state officer. For that, he has several possible solutions to improve the financing for BPA events.



Opportunities

Opportunities are an area Praveen hopes to expand at the state level. At the national level, volunteering and internships are available along with Certification testing. Those opportunities allow delegates to gain more experience as a business professional and are something Praveen wants to push for.



Communication

Communication is another major area Praveen sees that can be improved. Being more transparent with advisors and chapters allows delegates to have better opportunities and be more informed. Praveen also wants to foster closer connections between delegates and the officer team to both support delegates and communicate ideas.



Cooperation

Praveen's experiences communicating with delegates from other states as well as the national officers has introduced him to new ideas and policies. That is why he believes in cooperation between states to share ideas and policies to better improve the organization at the state level and foster improved relationships.



Participation

Praveen seeks to expand the middle level and garner more interest for BPA in regions lacking many chapters and participation. In doing so, he wants to make it more accessible to students across the state to pursue their interests and gain experience as business professionals.
