Projects



During this year, Withanage has worked on several projects, two of which harness his skills in software development to help his local chapter.





Proj. 1: A website that provides a generalized location for all information, documentation, and announcements regarding BPA information. It also has integrations for Google Forms, Spreadsheets, and Slides to streamline communications and data, lessening the stress of managing the chapter. Access Project

Withanage was inspired to build this website over the summer after seeing issues with communicating information and accessing documents from the previous year of BPA.







Proj. 1: A web application that allows local advisors to manage their students at conferences. It allows students to enter their status as "Away" or "Here," representing their location relative to the main conference area. It reduces the strain on advisors to manage their students' safety and location. Access Project

Withanage was inspired to build this web app after his advisor mentioned how stressful it was to manage all of her students at SLC given that some failed to notify her when they left the main conference area.
